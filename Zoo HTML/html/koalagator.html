Koalagator
This is one animal I'm very excited to introduce. It's unique to our zoo, never been seen before. Or rather, it would be unique to our zoo if we could get koalas and alligators to breed.

The issue isn't compatability, our top scientists did multiple compatability tests for the animals on LoveQuiz.com, and never got lower than 90%. The issue is that koalas are racist.

You wouldn't have thought it, looking at them. I mean, they're so fluffy! But it's true. The alligators were friendly and respectful, but the koalas... awful beasts. Enforcing unfair stereotypes, claiming the alligators were going to eat them. Honestly, if I were the alligator, I'd have eaten at least one koala. Maybe I still will. But they didn't, because they're good creatures. It's just so sad to see.

This zoo does not exist. Or does it? I mean, if you believe the multiverse theory, everything exists. In one universe, it might actually be good. Maybe. Hopefully.
