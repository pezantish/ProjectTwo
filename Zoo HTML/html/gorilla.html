Gorilla
Look at that majestic beast. Who doesn't like a gorilla? Well, I'll tell you who. Tarzan.

"But they're his family", you say. Well, that's exactly my point. Who actually likes their family? We tolerate them. We love them, sure, it's a moral obligation, but like? They're awful. You're forced to live with them for years on end, putting up with the yelling, the crying, the constant annoyances.

Can you really like a brother, let's say, who took your favourite teddy (let's call him Tom Bear, just hypothetically,) and ripped his legs off? I don't think so. And it doesn't matter that it's 20 years later, Mum, or that it was an accident, he hasn't shown any remorse.

Phew. I'm sorry about that. Who needs therapy, amirite? Anyway, gorillas. Pretty cool.
