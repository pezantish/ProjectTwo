Giraffe
Giraffes. Hideous creatures, with those obnoxiously long necks. I'm certain that if they possessed the ability to talk, that's all they'd talk about.

It's like those people that become parents, and never bloody shut up about it. Except instead of babies, it'd be their neck. "Awww, your grandma fell out of a tree? If I'd have been there, I could have saved her with my long neck. She'd still be alive today if I was there".

Yeah, I don't just think they'd be obnoxious, but narcissistic too, and sociopathic. They'd laugh behind your back if you were crying about said grandmother. Heck, maybe they put her in the tree in the first place, there's no way she managed with her legs the way they were. Evil beasts.

This zoo does not exist. Or does it? I mean, if you believe the multiverse theory, everything exists. In one universe, it might actually be good. Maybe. Hopefully.
